- Fork the project
- ![fork](https://cloud.githubusercontent.com/assets/286966/9465819/2e2fda74-4b30-11e5-9311-3838cbdc07db.png)

- Stay up to date with focus , read this (article)[https://help.github.com/articles/syncing-a-fork/]
